Our Delivery Process

We offer a diverse selection of milk, categorized into three distinct types: regular milk, children's milk, and plant-based milk. Customers have the option to select their desired milk variant and add it to their cart for checkout. During the checkout process, customers are prompted to specify their preferred delivery time, ensuring convenience and timely service


Our commitment to quality and safety is paramount. Our dedicated team of delivery personnel, whom we affectionately refer to as 'Lecheros', undergo rigorous training to ensure the safe and hygienic delivery of your order. Each delivery is meticulously handled to guarantee freshness and cleanliness.


As a token of our appreciation for their service, our Lecheros utilize carts to transport your milk bottles to your doorstep. We kindly request customers to return the cart outside their residence for collection the following day, thereby facilitating a seamless delivery process for all.


We extend our gratitude to our valued customers for their patronage and for maintaining a healthy relationship with our Lecheros. Rest assured, we remain steadfast in our commitment to delivering 'freshness at your doorstep' with every order.
